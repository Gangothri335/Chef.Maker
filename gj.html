LETS MAKE GULAB JAMUN

  1. Take The Gulab Jamun Floor.
  2. Pour Some Water And Make it as Dough.
  3. Make Dough As balls.
  4. Now Keep A Bowl On Stove And fill A Cup Of Sugar And Water.
  5. Make It As Thick Sugar Syrup.
  6. Now Fry The Maked Balls.
  7. Add All Fried Balls To The Sugar Syrup.
  8. Leave It For 20mins.
  9. Serve with Badam Or Garnish It As You Like.
